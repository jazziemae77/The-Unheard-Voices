988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

Focused mainly on suicide prevention, but also working with other mental health crises, this is the biggest mental health hotline in the U.S. The three digit number is easy to remember when you need it, and it is fully staffed 24/7, with generally very short wait times.

https://988lifeline.org/

How to contact: call or text 988, or visit their online chat service.

SAMHSA National Helpline

This hotline from the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA) isn't geared towards people actively in crisis like the others on this list; instead, this helpline provides useful information on mental health and refers callers to local treatment centers, support groups, and other community-based organizations.

https://www.samhsa.gov/find-help

How to contact: call 1-800-662-4357, text your zip code to 435748, or use their online treatment locator if you'd like to find help without a message.

  • Text
  • Online Treatment Finder
